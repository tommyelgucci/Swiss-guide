## What LAMal is

LAMal (basic health insurance) is Switzerland's mandatory health coverage.
Unlike many countries, there is no universal public system: everyone must
contract their own policy with a licensed private insurer.

## Deadlines

You have **3 months** from your arrival (or the start of your permit) to
arrange coverage. If you're late, coverage is still backdated to day one, but
you'll owe the missed months' premiums in a lump sum.

## Choosing an insurer

- Compare deductibles and premiums: a higher deductible means a lower monthly
  premium, but more out-of-pocket cost if you get sick.
- The basic LAMal coverage is legally identical across insurers; price and
  service are what differ.
- You can switch insurers once a year, usually before November 30 for the
  following year.
