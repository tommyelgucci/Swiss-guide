## The advantage of being an EU/EFTA citizen

Thanks to the Agreement on the Free Movement of Persons (AFMP) between
Switzerland and the EU, as a Spaniard you don't need a work visa and
you're not subject to the quotas (contingents) that apply to
third-country nationals.

## Permits by contract length

- **Permit L** (short-term): contracts under 1 year.
- **Permit B** (residence): contracts of 1 year or more, or open-ended.
- **Permit C** (settlement): after 5 or 10 years of continuous
  residence, depending on the canton.

## Procedure

Registration happens at the town hall (Gemeinde/Commune) of your place
of residence within 14 days of your arrival. You can start working
before you have the physical card, using the registration confirmation.
